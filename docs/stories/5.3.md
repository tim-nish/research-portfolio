# Story 5.3: Mode split integration — site-canonical and external articles in index and feeds

Canonical discussion record: https://github.com/tim-nish/website/issues/67
(part of umbrella #64 — `docs/article-publishing-spec.md` §1, two-mode
content model). On conflict, the issue wins.

As a reader, I want the writing index, home "recent writing", and feeds to
list both article modes coherently, so that every article appears exactly
once with the correct link target.

## Acceptance criteria

1. **Given** a mix of site-canonical and external articles, **when** the
   writing index, home section, and feeds are built, **then** site-canonical
   entries link to the on-site page and external entries stay link-out
   records (title, canonical URL, date) exactly as built in #24/#27.
2. **Given** any article, **when** listed anywhere, **then** it appears
   exactly once — mode is exclusive per article.
3. **Given** feed generation, **when** entries are emitted, **then**
   site-canonical entries may carry body/summary and external entries never
   carry a body.
4. **Given** an index containing both modes, **when** rendered, **then**
   link targets are correct and no duplicate entries exist.
