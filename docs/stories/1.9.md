# Story 1.9: RSS/Atom feed

**Status**: Draft
**Epic**: 1 — Hub Foundation
**Depends on**: 1.1

## Story

As a follower of the owner's work, I want an RSS/Atom feed, so that I can subscribe
without visiting the site directly.

## Acceptance criteria

1. `/feed.xml` is a valid RSS or Atom feed, generated at build time from the content
   registry (FR8).
2. In Phase 1 scope, the feed covers canonical articles — which don't exist yet until
   Epic 2 — so at Phase-1 completion the feed validates but may legitimately be empty
   of items, or (implementer's discretion, spec allows) include new projects/
   publications as an interim signal. **Decision recorded, not left ambiguous**: this
   story ships an empty-but-valid feed; Story 2.4 adds article items when the
   `article` type lands, so no rework of the feed generator is required later
   (NFR8).
3. Feed validates against a standard RSS/Atom validator.

## Tasks / subtasks

- [ ] Build the feed generator as a build-time step, parameterized by "which record
      types feed this feed" so Story 2.4 only adds a type, not new logic.
- [ ] Validate output against a standard feed validator (manual or scripted check).

## Dev notes

- Spec §8.3 says feed content is "at implementer's discretion" for
  full-text-vs-summary — this story picks summary+link (lighter, consistent with
  §8.5's transfer budget) and records that choice here rather than leaving it
  unstated.
